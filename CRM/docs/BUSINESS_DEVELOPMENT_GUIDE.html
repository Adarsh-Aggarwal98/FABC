Objection Handling

How to address common concerns

"We're too small for this"
Our Starter plan is designed specifically for practices your size. Even solo practitioners see ROI from reduced admin time and zero missed deadlines.
"We already have a system"
Many of our best customers switched from other tools. We offer free data migration and a 30-day trial so you can compare side-by-side with no risk.
"It looks complicated"
We designed this for accountants, not IT experts. Most practices are fully operational within a week, and we provide free onboarding support.
"What about our existing data?"
We provide professional data migration services. Your client history, documents, and records all come with you.
"How secure is it?"
We use bank-level encryption, Australian data centers, and comply with ATO digital requirements. Your data is safer with us than on a local computer.
"Can clients really use this?"
Absolutely. The client portal is deliberately simple. If your clients can use internet banking, they can use our portal.
