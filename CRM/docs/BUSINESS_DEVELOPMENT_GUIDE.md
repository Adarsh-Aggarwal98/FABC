# Accountant CRM - Business Development Guide

## Product Overview

**Accountant CRM** is a purpose-built practice management solution designed specifically for accounting firms, tax practices, and financial service providers. Unlike generic CRM systems, our platform understands the unique workflows, compliance requirements, and client management needs of the accounting profession.

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Pain Points for Accounting Practices

| Challenge | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| **Scattered Client Data** | Client information spread across emails, spreadsheets, and filing cabinets |
| **Manual Job Tracking** | No visibility into job status, deadlines, or workload distribution |
| **Lost Revenue** | Unbilled work, missed renewals, and forgotten follow-ups |
| **Compliance Risk** | Difficulty tracking lodgement deadlines and regulatory requirements |
| **Staff Inefficiency** | Time wasted on admin tasks instead of billable client work |
| **Client Communication Gaps** | No centralized system for queries, documents, and updates |
| **External Accountant Complexity** | B2B clients managing multiple entities with changing contacts |

## Key Features & Benefits

### 1. Client & Entity Management

**Feature:** Comprehensive client profiles with support for multiple entity types (Companies, Trusts, SMSFs, Partnerships, Individuals)

**Benefits:**
- Single source of truth for all client information
- Track multiple entities per client (perfect for external accountants managing portfolios)
- Historical contact tracking - never lose data when a POC changes
- Australian business identifiers (ABN, ACN, TFN) built-in
- Trust-specific fields (trust type, trustee details, deed dates)

**Value Proposition:** *"Know your clients better than ever before. All information, all entities, all history - in one place."*

---

### 2. Service Request & Job Management

**Feature:** End-to-end job tracking from request to completion

**Benefits:**
- Automated request numbering and tracking
- Visual workflow management with customizable stages
- Priority and deadline management
- Workload visibility across the team
- Assignment and reassignment with full audit trail
- State-based duration tracking for process improvement

**Value Proposition:** *"Never miss a deadline. Never lose track of a job. Full visibility from request to completion."*

---

### 3. Invoicing & Revenue Tracking

**Feature:** Integrated invoicing with professional PDF generation

**Benefits:**
- Create invoices directly from completed jobs
- Customizable invoice templates with your branding
- GST/Tax calculations built-in
- Payment tracking and reminders
- Revenue vs. cost analytics for profitability insights
- Multi-currency support for international clients

**Value Proposition:** *"Get paid faster. Track every dollar. Understand your true profitability."*

---

### 4. Document Management

**Feature:** Secure cloud-based document storage linked to clients and jobs

**Benefits:**
- Centralized document repository
- Direct upload from client portal
- Document versioning and history
- Secure sharing links
- Integration with cloud storage providers
- Organized by client, entity, and job

**Value Proposition:** *"All your documents, securely stored, instantly accessible."*

**Benefits:**

**After:**

- Zero missed deadlines

### Use Case 2: Growing Bookkeeping Firm

**Scenario:** 5-person team handling BAS for 50 business clients

**Before:** Jobs tracked in shared spreadsheet, no visibility into workload, inconsistent processes

**After:**
- Standardized workflow for BAS preparation
- Balanced workload across team members
- Automated reminders for quarterly lodgements
- 40% improvement in turnaround time
- Client satisfaction up significantly

## Getting Started - Sales Process

### Discovery Questions

1. **Current Tools:** "What systems do you currently use to manage clients and jobs?"
2. **Pain Points:** "What's your biggest frustration with your current process?"
3. **Growth Plans:** "How many clients do you expect to have in 2 years?"
4. **Team Size:** "How many people need access to client information?"
5. **Compliance:** "How do you currently track lodgement deadlines?"
6. **Client Types:** "Do you manage trusts, SMSFs, or companies for clients?"

### Qualification Criteria

**Good Fit:**
- Managing 50+ clients
- 2+ team members OR solo wanting to grow
- Currently using spreadsheets or basic tools
- Pain around job tracking or client communication
- Values efficiency and professionalism

**Not Ideal Fit:**
- Very small practices content with current process
- Already invested in enterprise solutions
- No interest in digital transformation

### Demo Script Outline

1. **Show Client Management** (2 min) - Create a client, show entity types
2. **Create a Service Request** (2 min) - Show client portal view
3. **Job Workflow** (3 min) - Show assignment, status updates, deadlines
4. **Generate Invoice** (2 min) - Show PDF, branding
5. **Dashboard & Analytics** (2 min) - Show value of visibility
6. **Q&A** (5+ min) - Address specific needs
